Survive:

The primary objective of a space shooter game is to survive as long as possible by avoiding enemy attacks and obstacles.

Defeat enemies:

The player must shoot down enemy spaceships and other threats to progress through levels and advance in the game.

Upgrade:

The player can collect power-ups and upgrade their ship's weapons, shields, and other features to become more powerful and better equipped to face tougher enemies.

Boss battles:

Space shooter games often include challenging boss battles, which require the player to use advanced tactics and strategies to defeat them.

Exploration:

Some space shooter games feature exploration elements, where the player can discover new planets, weapons, and other secrets.

High score:

Space shooter games often have a high score system, where players can compete with each other for the highest score.



The main objective of a space shooter game is to provide a thrilling, action-packed experience that challenges the player's skills and reflexes while also providing a sense of adventure and exploration in outer space.
